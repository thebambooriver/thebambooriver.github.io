About
the Firm

Established in 1987,CHRISTIE SOOSAY NATHAN & ASSOCIATES is a boutique law firm which specialises in both dispute resolution and advisory work. This encompasses legal practice areas spanning from high end civil litigation, corporate, constuction, employment/industrial relations and family law disputes.

We provide advisory services in the areas of corporate commercial transactions, construction contracts, and regulatory and public policy matters. Through the years the firm's clientelle has evolved from only individual and small businesses to presently include public listed and multinational corporations.

Our clients are our priority, and every client be they individual or corporate, large or small, is given our fullest attention and priority. We analyse cases from the outset to convey to clients the available courses of action, alternatives to litigation and towards this end, develop case plans and strategies.

Meet Our
Team

Christie graduated with honours from the University of Malaya in 1983 and was admitted as an advocate and solicitor of the High court of Malaya in 1984. After three years of legal practice, two of which were in one of the leading law firms in Malaysia, during which formative years he conducted both trials and appeals before the High Court and the Federal Court.

He then established the firm in 1987 and takes an active hand in each file taken on by the firm. Given his wide exposure to the various areas of the law he appears in the Superior Courts and arbitration proceedings. His specialities include industrial and employnent law, construction,commercial and family law disputes. He further provides advisory assistance to clients in these areas of the law.
